Trade Control POS

Trade Control is a web-based Point of Sale (POS) application designed for internal inventory management and daily sales tracking.

How it works: The application requires access to your Google Sheets. It reads product data (names, prices, barcodes) from your personal spreadsheet to display them in the POS interface, and writes completed sales transactions directly back into your spreadsheet.
