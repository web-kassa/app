Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Trade Control application (hereinafter "the App") collects, uses, and handles your data. The App is a web-based POS (Point of Sale) terminal designed for inventory management and sales tracking.

1. Google Workspace APIs Data Usage

To function properly, Trade Control requests access to your Google Sheets (Google Sheets API). This access is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Reading Data: The App reads information about your products, prices, and inventory levels from your connected database (Google Sheets) to display them in the POS interface.
  • Writing Data: The App writes information about completed transactions (sales, refunds) directly into your Google Spreadsheet.

2. Limited Use Policy

Trade Control's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. Data Storage and Transfer

Trade Control operates on a client-server architecture communicating directly with Google's infrastructure. The App does not have its own third-party servers or hidden databases.

  • We do not store, copy, or transfer your commercial data, product lists, or transaction history to any third-party servers.
  • All your data physically remains and is stored exclusively on your personal Google Drive account (within your Google Sheets).
  • We do not sell or share your data with any third parties.

4. Security

Because all data is processed and stored within your Google account, its security is guaranteed by Google Workspace's standard protection and encryption mechanisms. The App uses a secure HTTPS protocol for all communications.

5. Your Rights and Data Management

You are the full owner of your data. You can revoke the App's access to your Google account at any time via your Google Account security settings ("Third-party apps with account access"). You can also delete your Google Sheets containing the records at any time.
